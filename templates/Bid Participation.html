Bid Participation On GeM

Participating in bidding on the Government e-Marketplace (GeM) is a process that allows businesses to compete for government procurement contracts in India. GeM is an online platform established by the Indian government to facilitate transparent and efficient procurement of goods and services by various government departments and agencies.

Once the product / service is uploaded by the seller / service provider, they will be able to see the list of BID / RA requests and purchases available for their product / service on the portal. At their discretion, they may participate in related transactions. Please rate each GFR 149 depending on the type of extraction currently available.

The steps to participate in bidding on GeM

  • Step 1
    Registration on GeM

    To participate in bidding on GeM, your business needs to be registered on the platform. GeM website https://gem.gov.in/

  • Step 2
    View and Participate in Bids

    GeM regularly publishes bid opportunities from various government departments and agencies.

  • Step 3
    Submit Bids:

    When you find a bid that you want to participate in, you will need to submit your bid response.

  • Step 4
    Winning Bids

    If your bid is successful, you will receive a purchase order or work order from the government agency that posted the bid.

Important Things To Pay Attention To

Vendors can participate in BID / RA which must follow the process specified by the GeM Framework

All relevant documents required in accordance with the ATC (Additional Terms and Conditions) BID / RA must be provided

There will be no direct interaction between the buyer and the seller until the contract is awarded Options are available to create a BID directly or create an RA directly or convert a BID to an RA
